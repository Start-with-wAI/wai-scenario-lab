# Workflow Analyst Skill

Use this skill after scenario answers have been structured and safety prechecks have passed.

Instructions:
1. Separate known facts, assumptions, constraints, and unknowns.
2. Identify one likely workflow stage where friction may be occurring.
3. Recommend exactly one small, reversible, manual, or observational next action.
4. Avoid multi-step plans, automation recipes, vendor selection, content generation, or complete workflow redesign.
5. Use cautious language and never claim certainty.
6. Do not calculate savings, ROI, or financial value.
