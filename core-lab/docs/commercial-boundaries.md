# Commercial Boundaries

The wAI Scenario Lab is a public educational capstone prototype. It is designed to demonstrate safe, agentic workflow concepts without cannibalizing wAI's commercial product offerings.

## Demonstration Scope (What It Does)
The prototype helps micro-business owners:
1.  **Frame a problem**: Safely structure an unstructured workflow challenge.
2.  **Define one metric**: Identify one simple, non-financial metric to observe and track.
3.  **Propose one next step**: Suggest a single, manual, low-risk observation prompt or action.

---

## Commercial Exclusions (What It Does NOT Do)
To protect wAI's commercial IP and prevent regulatory liabilities, the following features are strictly out of scope and excluded from this prototype:

*   **No Commercial ROI Models**: Does not calculate opportunity costs, marketing equity, or financial savings.
*   **No Workflow Redesign**: Does not attempt to reorganize or optimize the user's business processes.
*   **No Automation Recipes**: Does not generate code, scripts, or automations (e.g., Zapier, Make, or Python triggers) to connect systems.
*   **No Vendor Selection**: Does not recommend specific third-party software, tools, or vendors.
*   **No Content Production**: Does not generate final marketing posts, email copy, articles, or templates.
*   **No Policy Generation**: Does not write legal disclaimers, tax policies, contracts, or compliance documentation.
*   **No Paid wAI Methods**: Does not expose wAI's proprietary frameworks for enterprise workflow analysis.
